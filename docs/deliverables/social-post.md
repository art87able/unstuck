Built Unstuck for #BuildSmallHackathon: paste one overwhelming task, get tiny timed steps — and only the next one is lit up. No wall of tasks.

It times each step for you (Start → Done), learns your personal time-blindness from the actuals, and recalibrates future estimates "for you" — with the receipts visible in a per-category history.

Your plans and time data live in your browser, not on a server. Runs on a ≤4B model (`Qwen/Qwen3-4B-Instruct-2507`) you can host yourself.

Breakdown bots tell you what to do. Unstuck learns how long *you* actually take.

Try it: https://huggingface.co/spaces/build-small-hackathon/unstuck
Source: https://github.com/art87able/unstuck
